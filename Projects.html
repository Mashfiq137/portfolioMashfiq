CovidVM- Diagnosis of Covid infected x-ray Images

(Feature extraction, Densenet169, Tensorflow, Sci-kit learn, Python)
December 2020

More than 26 million cases of Corona virus disease (COVID-19) in the world so far has been identified. The outbreak of this Extreme Acute Respiratory Syndrome Coronavirus has caused many deaths. In order to control the spread of the disease, screening huge quantity of suspected cases for appropriate quarantine and treatment are the highest priority. Pathogenic lab testing is typically the gold standard, but it bears the burden of significant false negativity and as well as human-made errors, adding to the urgent need of alternative & new diagnostic methods to combat the disease. Based on COVID-19 radiographic changes in X-ray images, this project implies that artificial intelligence methods might be able to extract specific image features of COVID-19 and provide a clinical diagnosis ahead of the pathogenic test, thus saving critical time & life-risks for disease control. A multiclass classification technique was used to identify the Covid x-ray images from pneumonia affected and normal patients. CovidVm is a combination of a neural network and support vector machine classifier. The features that were extracted from the images of the X-ray, were used to train the model. The model gave 97% validation accuracy with impressive f1-score.

Fuel Usage Monitoring(FUM): a diesel theft prevention device for large container ships.

(C++, Arduino ESP8266, Flow Sensor, AWS)
January 2020

Theft of diesel and petrol is a huge problem in the Bangladeshi shipping industry. The emergence of a syndicate around the shipping industry has made it tougher for the owners of the ships to carry on their business over the last two decades. The syndicate consists of the staff members of the ships and the fuel supplier. Consistently, the syndicate tries to make money out of the shipowners by less amount of diesel supplied into their ships. The kind of money they make out of this creates huge losses for the owners. This problem not only exists in Bangladesh but also in India. To prevent this kind of malfeasance, I have made a fuel monitoring system that enables the owners to know the quantity of fuel that is being used by the engine of the ship. The owner of the ships can remotely get all the information about the fuel usage through the internet. The hardware end of the project uses a "Fuel Flow Meter" and a micro-computer(Arduino or Raspberry) to gather all the information from the ship. On the other hand, the owner gets all the information from a mobile application. Additionally, sudden changes in the flow will trigger an alarm and an alert will be sent to the owner automatically.

Combinational & Sequential circuit implementation to display particular texts

(Software: Logisim)
(Hardware: Breadboard, AND-gate, OR-gate, NOT-gate, Flipflops, Clock)
Spring 2018

The main idea of the project is project is to deploy a total circuit which will automatically show CSE231123 in single 7-segment display. It can be in both forward or reverse (321132ESC) way. Total design of this circuit will be designed in three phases. In the first phase, we will design the combinational part of the circuit, which means we will specify our inputs and what will be the outputs for the corresponding outputs for the circuit and we will go on formulation step. After formulating them, we will further implement the circuit with our theoretical simplified designed, and finally check and verify if we are getting all the expected outputs for the inputs as per defined in the “specification” step, also our desired one. Later, we will design the sequential circuit with the facilitation of forward and reverse sequence. Further, the sequence will be implemented according to prior state diagram design according to the procedure.
